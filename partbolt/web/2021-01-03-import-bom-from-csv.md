# Import a BOM from CSV
You can now import a Bill of Materials (BOM) from external software directly into a PartBolt project. It's fast and easy! From your project's items list, just click the **<i class="fas fa-fw fa-file-import"></i> Import CSV** button and follow the prompts.

For more information, see [our Help Center article on the topic](https://help.partbolt.com/articles/import-bom-from-csv).
