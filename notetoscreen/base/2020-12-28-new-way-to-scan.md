# A new way to scan
In the latest version of the NoteToScreen app, we've introduced a whole new way to scan notebook pages! Just point your phone at a page. We'll automatically find its corners and crop it for you&mdash;all you have to do is press "Done".

Try it now! Get the NoteToScreen app at the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/notetoscreen/id1292401613) or the [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.notetoscreen.android).
